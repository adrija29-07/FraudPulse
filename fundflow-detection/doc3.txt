1. Why Typography Matters for This Dashboard
The PS3 Fund Flow Tracking platform handles two very different cognitive tasks at the same time. Analysts need to read large financial figures at a glance under pressure, and also read dense investigative text and table data during long case review sessions. A single font cannot serve both jobs equally well.
The four-font stack below was chosen by matching visual patterns directly observed in the reference screenshots ù the dark finance dashboard and the AceDeal light UI ù and mapping each font to a specific cognitive role in the interface.

2. Font 1 ù Syne (Display & Headings)
Fund Flow
Syne 800 ù Display
Syne is a geometric display typeface designed for impactful headlines. Its ultra-bold 800 weight produces the same aggressive visual authority as the large titles in Image 1 ('Premium Plan', 'Home') and the AceDeal logo. It signals that this is a serious, professional platform ù not a generic data tool.
